:: The Slip ::

This week, I had a bit of an incident: I was riding my scooter after the rain, and the wheel slipped on the tram tracks. It went into a drift, and the scooter flew out from under my feet. I had to run after it for a few meters before the scooter finally came to a stop. It happened so fast and unexpectedly that I didn’t even have time to get scared. The “wait, what just happened?” realization only hit me after the incident was already over 🥴 It was dangerous, but luckily there were no bruises. I think I need to avoid tram tracks in wet weather.
